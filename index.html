White Beef Matton

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef, cut into small cubes
  • 4 onions, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 cup water
  • 4 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot and saut√© the onions until they become translucent.
  2. Add the garlic, ginger paste, coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric powder, and garam masala to the pot and stir well.
  3. Add the beef and salt to the pot and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Beat the yogurt in a bowl and add it to the pot. Mix well and cook for another 10 minutes.
  5. Add the water to the pot and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 45 minutes or until the beef is tender and the gravy thickens.
  6. Remove from heat and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy your delicious White Beef Matton!
